# AI Infra Repository Instructions

When a conversation about this repository's AI infrastructure curriculum produces a durable mechanism, correction, reproducible observation, misconception, dependency, priority change, or independently learnable leaf, use the repository-local skill `.codex/skills/infra-learning-capture` before the final response.

Apply its capture workflow by default. When a target leaf or its scaffold-owned files are absent, create the leaf or run `python3 tools/scaffold_leaf.py <leaf>` yourself before writing the AI-owned material. Preserve learner-owned `note.md`; write reusable course material to `tutorial.md` or `lesson.md`, discussion-complete review material to `review.md`, evidence to labs, retrieval prompts to `questions.md`, and cross-domain navigation to `atlas/`.
